Introduction

In 2020, after several years of being in a bit of a plateau with my musical skills, I set a New Year's Resolution to practice music in some form every day. I started tracking what I was practicing in early March as a way to keep track of my progress.

Some additional notes:

  • A song is considered practiced if I practice for more than ~ 15 minutes. Basically I need to make meaningful progress into playing it better for it to count. But beyond that, time spent practicing is not included. The time spent praciticing a song in a given day could be 15 minutes or 4 hours.
  • Genre is subjective. A lot of artists toe the line between genres. I'm really just curious about overall trends so if I call an artist "rock" when they're really "metal"... oh well.
